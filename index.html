Go-To-Market Strategy Guide

A four-step framework for launching your import business.

Step 1: Sourcing & Validation

Research and identify reliable suppliers on platforms like Alibaba or 1688.com. Always order product samples to rigorously test quality, functionality, and packaging before placing a bulk order. Negotiate pricing based on volume.

1

Step 2: Logistics & Import

Partner with a reputable freight forwarder to handle shipping from China to Bangladesh. Ensure you have the correct documentation (Bill of Lading, Commercial Invoice, Packing List) and understand the customs duties and taxes for your products to avoid delays.

2

Step 3: Marketing & Branding

Build your online presence. Create a brand page on Facebook and Instagram. Use high-quality photos and videos of your products. Run targeted ads to reach your ideal customer segments. List your products on local marketplaces like Daraz.com.bd.

3

Step 4: Sales & Fulfillment

Manage orders efficiently. Offer cash-on-delivery as a primary payment option. Partner with reliable local courier services (e.g., Pathao, REDX) to ensure timely and professional delivery to customers across Bangladesh. Good customer service is key to repeat business.

4
